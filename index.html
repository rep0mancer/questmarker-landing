Features

Built for Real Life

📋
Full Task Editor

Title, notes, objectives, attachments, due dates, reminders, repeat rules, priority & categories.

🔔
Smart Reminders

Local notifications scheduled per quest. Never miss a mission-critical deadline.

♻️
Trash & Restore

Accidentally deleted? Restore from trash within the session or permanently purge.

💾
Persistent Storage

All quests saved locally. Your data stays on your device — no cloud required.

🎨
Skin Independence

Create a quest in one skin — it's fully visible and editable in every other skin too.

⚡
Haptic Feedback

Satisfying completion animations with skin-specific haptics. Every quest feels earned.
